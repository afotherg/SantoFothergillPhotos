Hey, I'm Santo!

Photography is one of my greatest passions. There's something magical about freezing a single moment in time and turning it into something you can hold onto forever. I'm especially drawn to landscape photography -- there's nothing quite like chasing the perfect light across mountain ridges, coastlines, and open skies.


My dream is to pursue photography full-time, and I'm well on my way. I currently offer professional headshots, graduation portraits, and engagement sessions -- and I'm excited to be expanding into wedding photography. Whether it's a polished corporate headshot, a fun group celebration, or a once-in-a-lifetime surprise proposal, I bring the same energy, care, and creative eye to every shoot.

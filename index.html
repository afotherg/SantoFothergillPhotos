Hey, I'm Santo!

Ever since I was young, I have been in love with talking photos. It started with just taking photos of random stuff I found interesting on the streets to then growing with my passion of The Outdoors and capturing moments with my friends. Photography gives me a chance to show off my creative talent. Capturing the sunset disappearing over the ocean, the view from the top of a 14er, or a photo of one of my friends smiling is one of my greatest joys.My dream for this website is to become to a place to connect with people who want there greatest moments captured as well as share stories from my life and adventures.


My dream is to be able to do photography full time. To be able to use my skills to travel the world, sleep under the stars and capture every moment in between. To help achieve this goal over the last couple of years I have been working on my craft doing professional headshots, graduation portraits, and engagement sessions. This is something I hope to be able to continue at a larger scale as well as delving into the world of wedding photos and selling prints of my work. I hope to be able to use my photography to inspire more happiness in people's lives! 😊
